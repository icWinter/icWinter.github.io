Merlin

《Merlin》(梅林传奇)
In a land of myth and a time of magic,The destiny of a great kingdom rests on the shoulders of a yong man , His name… Merlin .

I’ve to say , it’s amazing story , sorcery was not allowed in Camelot , but Camelot cannot live without it . sorcery can be used to heal things more than destroy things , It’s not terrible thing for sorcery , the point is a man who use it.

Destiny is the the worse things, No one can get rid of it when you know your destiny , Anyone try to do stop it will lead to the destiny coming, it got nothing changed at last .

Some men are born to plough fields , some to be great physicians , others to be great kings . Me , I was born to serve you , Arthur . And I’m proud of it . And I wouldn’t change a thing.

Though no man , no matter how great , can know his destiny , some lives have been foretold . Arthur is not just a king , he is the Once and Feature King . For when Albion’s need is greatest , Arthur will rise again.
